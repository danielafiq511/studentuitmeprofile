Universiti Teknologi Mara

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is the largest comprehensive university in Malaysia providing innovative education with state-of-the-art infrastructure and technology within reach at its 34 campuses, 4 colleges of studies, 14 faculties, and 9 academic centres all over the country.

Academic Progress

Learning 1

Database Management in UiTM equips students with the knowledge and skills to design, develop, and manage databases effectively. It covers core concepts like data models, database management systems (DBMS), and entity-relationship modeling, along with SQL for data manipulation. Students learn about database architecture, security, and data integrity while applying their knowledge to practical projects. The course prepares them for careers in database administration, software development, and data analysis.

30%
Learning 2

Web Development in UiTM focuses on teaching students to design and build dynamic, user-friendly websites and web applications. The course covers key topics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and web frameworks, along with responsive design, front-end, and back-end development. Students gain hands-on experience in creating functional web solutions while learning about web hosting, deployment, and optimization. This subject prepares students for careers in web design, development, and related digital fields.

15%
Learning 3

Problem Solving in UiTM teaches students critical thinking and analytical skills to identify, analyze, and resolve real-world problems effectively. The subject emphasizes systematic approaches like defining problems, brainstorming solutions, decision-making, and evaluating outcomes. Students learn techniques such as flowcharting, algorithms, and logical reasoning, often applying these skills in practical scenarios or projects. This course prepares students to tackle challenges in academic, professional, and everyday contexts.

50%
